Hi, I'm Simon from Team Krypton. Team Krypton was founded in 2022, although I've been building simple line followers with a Lego Mindstorms Ev3 since 2018.
We participated in RoboCup for the first time in February 2023; you can find all our runs on the EvB RoboCup YouTube channel. We achieved 3rd place, which unfortunately did not qualify us for the German Open in Kassel.
After RoboCup 2023, my friend left the team, but two others joined. We qualified for the German Open 2024 in Kassel and secured 42nd place.
Although we had hoped for more, we successfully used a Raspberry Pi in combination with a Pi camera to follow the line, which was our main goal for 2024.

For 2025, we plan to improve everything, including collecting victims in the evacuation zone and much more...
So stay tuned!
